About

This site was built by Alec Gorge. He also built PhishOD, the Listen to the Dead iOS App, and helps out over at phish.in. Alec loves live music and he was disappointed that were weren't sites like phish.in or phishtracks.com for Lotus, Marco Benevento and STS9.

He spent many hours listening to them in the Live Music Archives of archive.org and figured there had to be a better way so he built a few websites (iOS apps coming soon!):

  • LotusOD.com
  • MarcoAllDay.com
  • STS9OD.com

All the music that you stream comes from archive.org.

Donations

Do not donate to me. This is a labor of love. Please donate everything to archive.org. I merely reorganized the data to make it more easily consumable.

None of this is possible without the countless hours of hard work by the archive.org volunteers.
